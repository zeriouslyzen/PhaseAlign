## Why these differentiate

- **Conditions over compounds:** Focus is on tuning (dials, conditions), not "this ingredient fixes you."
- **You're the site:** Customer is where bioelectric/mechanical/environmental physics run — not a passive recipient.
- **We = tuners:** Phase Alignment is the one setting dials / conditions — expertise, precision, no fluff.
- **Intelligence in brevity:** Short lines leave room for the reader to fill in "why me / why now."

Pick one or mix elements (e.g. "You're the system. We set the dials." or "Stored potential. We tune it.") and we can tighten further.
